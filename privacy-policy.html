Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Welcome to Padelora: Padel Score App (the "App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect personally identifiable information (such as your real name, email, address, or phone number). Everything you enter in the App — including match scores, player names, tactic boards, chemistry ratings and personal preferences — is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

We may collect anonymized technical data required to operate the App and improve its quality, such as:

  • App version, device model and iOS version
  • Aggregated, non-identifying usage data (e.g., which features are opened)
  • Purchase events processed through RevenueCat
  • Crash and stability diagnostics

2. Live Activities and Notifications

If you start a match, the App may display a Live Activity on your Lock Screen and on the Dynamic Island. This content lives entirely on your device and is not transmitted to any external server. You can disable Live Activities at any time in Settings → Padelora → Live Activities, or globally in iPhone Settings.

3. Purchases and Payments

The App offers an optional premium subscription called Padelora Plus. Plus unlocks:

  • Unlimited match history and full match details
  • Advanced statistics — win rate, streaks, weekly activity and player chemistry analytics
  • Best teammate, favorite opponent and rank progression insights
  • Unlimited tactics board — create, save and share unlimited plays
  • Smart match insights and personalized recommendations
  • Save match cards and tactics to your Photos library

Padelora Plus is offered with the following auto-renewing options:

  • Monthly: $14.99 USD per month
  • Annual: $44.99 USD per year

Padelora Plus does not include a free trial. Subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing period unless canceled at least 24 hours before the period ends. You can manage or cancel a subscription at any time in your Apple ID Subscriptions settings.

Payments are processed securely by Apple. We do not collect, see or store your payment information. Subscription status is managed via RevenueCat, a third-party service that uses anonymous user identifiers to validate purchases and prevent fraud.

4. Photos Library Access

If you tap "Save to Photos" inside the Tactics Board or the Match Result screen, the App writes a rendered image to your Photos library. The App never reads or imports any other content from your Photos. You can revoke this permission at any time in iPhone Settings → Padelora → Photos.

5. Data Security

We follow industry-standard practices to ensure data integrity and security. Because the App stores your data locally and does not collect personal identifiers, privacy risks are minimized.

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent or share personal data with third parties. Anonymized purchase events and crash diagnostics may be shared with RevenueCat strictly to operate and support in-app purchases.

7. Your Rights

You can manage or restore your subscription at any time through your Apple ID Subscriptions settings.

You can erase all locally stored data at any time inside the App: Settings → Erase All Data.

You can disable Live Activities or notifications at any time in iPhone Settings.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years old and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
